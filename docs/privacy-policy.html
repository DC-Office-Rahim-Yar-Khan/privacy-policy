Introduction

At DC Office Rahim Yar Khan, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use the CM Punjab RYK Price Control App. By using our app, you agree to this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect the following personal and sensitive information to provide and improve our services:

  • Location Data: We collect your location data to provide services based on your location. Authorities can use this data to find the location where the complaint was submitted.
  • Name and Phone Number: We collect your name and phone number for identification and communication purposes when resolving complaints.
  • Images: Any images provided with your complaint submissions are stored and used solely for the purpose of resolving those complaints.
  • App Usage Information: We may collect non-personally identifiable information about your use of the app to improve its performance and user experience.

How We Use Your Data

We use your personal information to:

  • Process and resolve complaints efficiently.
  • Ensure accurate identification and communication with complainants.
  • Share relevant data with authorities and regulatory bodies to resolve the issue.
  • Improve app functionality and user experience.

Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your personal data very seriously. Your data is stored securely in Firebase Firestore and Firebase Storage, which employ industry-standard encryption. Only authorized personnel have access to your information, and we take all necessary measures to ensure that your data is protected against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your data in the following situations:

  • With law enforcement agencies or other authorities as required by law to investigate and resolve your complaint.
  • With third-party service providers to assist with app functionality, subject to strict data protection standards.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in our system.
  • Request the correction or deletion of your data.
  • Object to or restrict the processing of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details provided below.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
